About Me

Currently, I work as a Lecturer in the School of Computing and Creative Industries at Leeds Trinity University (LTU), U.K. Prior to joining Leeds Trinity University, I worked as Postdoctoral Research Associate in the Department of Engineering at Durham University, U.K., and as a Research Fellow in the School of Engineering at the University of Warwick, U.K. My career spans over 10 years, dedicated to Information Communications Technology (ICT). My expertise includes software development, integrated software and hardware products, self-organizing network systems, artificial intelligence, and wireless communications. Alongside my R&D work, I have extensive experience in tutoring and teaching, teaching material development, guiding students in exams, graduation projects, Master or PhD students’ supervision and entrepreneurship competitions. I also collaborate closely with industry, leading enterprise-level project development and technical guidance, including algorithm design and programming training in C, C++, C#, Java, Python, etc. My research interests include UAV communications, THz-integrated sensing, communications and powering, as well as wireless charging and communications.
